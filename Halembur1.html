Serving Authentic Tea Since 1985

Halembur Tea Stall began as a small family venture with a simple mission: to serve the finest tea using time-honored techniques and the highest quality ingredients. Today, we continue that legacy with unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our master tea makers travel across regions to source the most exquisite tea leaves, ensuring each blend offers a unique character and depth of flavor that discerning tea lovers appreciate.

We believe that tea is more than a beverage—it's a ritual, a moment of pause, and an opportunity to connect with others. This philosophy guides everything we do, from our careful brewing methods to our warm, inviting atmosphere.
